Target Audience Description

The target audience is broad, ranging in ages from 18- 100. The website will be accessed on various platforms namely mobile, tablet and desktop. The audience will compose of both business owners, and perspective owners seeking to advance their interests including but not limited to networking, planning, promoting and community service. The audience will also include local citizens, local government leaders and volunteers and perhaps those wishing to relocate and join the community.

Personas

Malcolm has grown up in Meadow Grove and is twenty-one years old. Malcolm just graduated with a bachelors in botany and is often in forests and meadows outlining the town collecting herbs, flowers, and minerals. He dislikes big box stores and has always preferred small storefronts and down to earth kind people. Currently he is working as a waiter in a small health food restaurant while completing a second degree in business management online. He is very comfortable navigating the web and knows his way around computers. He considers himself to be an expert at research as well as an expert in customer service. His passion is creating natural apothecary remedies and items to harness the power of nature and improve peoples quality of life.He is planning to open a small apothecary in the community. He has a love for this tight knit community and has noticed how the businesses uplift and support eachother. He is eager to join the Chamber of Commerce in order to network and gain the support of the business commuity

Addsion is a 65 year old woman who owns a local fabric and quilt shop. She has been a member of the chamber of commerce for 45 years. She had absouletly no computer experince when the web site was first built in 2003. She is still by no means an expert but she is active on the site and uses it to search for community events post her own events and keep up with what is going on with other busineses in the community. Addison will be soley be accesing the the site from her laptop as she only has a flip phone with no internet access.

Scenarios

  1. Malcolm wants to investigate becoming a member. He is looking for the cost, advantages, and requirmenetns to becoming a member.
